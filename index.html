About

I am currently a PhD student at the School of Artificial Intelligence, Jilin University (JLU), advised by Prof. Yixing Gao.

Prior to this, I obtained my master’s and bachelor’s degrees from the College of Computer Science and Technology (College of Data Science), Taiyuan University of Technology, advised by Prof. Daoxiang Zhou.

My current research interests are Computer Vision, Embodied Intelligence and Robotic Manipulation.
